The Alchemist

About : The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho is a philosophical novel that tells the story of Santiago, a young shepherd who embarks on a journey in search of treasure hidden in the Egyptian pyramids. Along the way, he encounters various characters and obstacles that teach him profound life lessons about the pursuit of one's dreams, self-discovery, and the spiritual nature of the universe. At its core, the novel conveys the message that when you follow your "Personal Legend" or true calling, the universe conspires to help you achieve it. The Alchemist is a timeless fable that inspires readers to pursue their passions and trust the journey, no matter how daunting it may seem.
