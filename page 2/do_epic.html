Do Epic Shit

About : Do Epic Shit by Ankur Warikoo is an inspiring self-help book that blends personal experiences with practical advice on living a purposeful and fulfilling life. Warikoo shares lessons learned from his entrepreneurial journey, touching on topics like failure, personal growth, goal-setting, and time management. His relatable anecdotes and direct, no-nonsense approach offer insights on how to overcome fear, embrace uncertainty, and make bold choices. The book emphasizes the importance of taking risks, cultivating a growth mindset, and making meaningful decisions. It encourages readers to create a life aligned with their passions and values, urging them to “do epic shit.”
